1. Processing Fees — Refundable Account Synchronization Fee

MetasBank charges a processing fee on all withdrawal requests. This fee is fully refundable and is classified as an Account Synchronization Fee. It covers the administrative cost of reconciling your account across our multi-layered verification and settlement systems.

Crypto Withdrawals
0.9% (min $2.00)
ACH Transfers
1.0% (min $3.00 / max $50.00)
Wire Transfers
1.5% (min $10.00 / max $500.00)
Refundable Fee Policy: The processing fee is refunded to your account upon successful synchronization of your withdrawal. If the withdrawal is rejected or cancelled, the full amount including the fee is returned to your balance. This ensures that you are only charged for successfully completed transactions.

By initiating a withdrawal, you acknowledge and agree that the processing fee will be held as a refundable deposit for account synchronization purposes. The fee will be released back to your account once the synchronization process is complete, as outlined in our Terms and Conditions.

4. Refundable Fee — Detailed Terms

4.1 What Is the Account Synchronization Fee?

The Account Synchronization Fee is a refundable deposit collected at the time of withdrawal. It is not a revenue charge but a temporary hold to facilitate the secure reconciliation of your withdrawal request across our internal and external settlement systems.

4.2 When Is the Fee Refunded?

  • Successful Withdrawal: The fee is refunded to your account within 24 hours of the withdrawal being marked as complete. Your net deduction is the withdrawal amount minus the refunded fee.
  • Rejected Withdrawal: The full withdrawal amount plus the fee is immediately restored to your balance.
  • Cancelled Withdrawal: Same as rejected — full restoration.

4.3 Why Is It Called "Account Synchronization"?

This fee covers the automated processes that ensure your account records are consistent across our database, payment processors, and banking partners. These processes include ledger reconciliation, transaction verification, and compliance screening.
